Instructions
  • Follow the line: Start from the left side and trace the line all the way to the right side of the page.
  • Crossing lines: The line might cross other lines and sometimes go back across the page.
  • Find the symbol: See where the line ends and identify the symbol there.
  • Check the cart: Look at the chart to find the letter that corresponds to the symbol.
  • Tick the box: Go back to the start and tick the box under the letter you found in the chart.
  • Work as quickly as you can.
  • Time limit: 2 minutes.
